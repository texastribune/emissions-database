Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 11/22/2016 6:48PM
This is based on the: INITIAL REPORT Event ended: 11/23/2016 8:49AM
Cause DURING NIGHT TIME OPERATIONS THE 101 INLET COMPRESSOR AT THE WELCH CO2 GAS PROCESSING FACILITY WAS TAKEN DOWN, ROUTING ITS INLET TO FLARE.
Action taken MALLET CONTROL ROOM RECEIVED AN ALARM THAT HIGH O2 LEVELS WERE COMING INTO THE WELCH CO2 GAS PROCESSING FACILITY. CALL OUT SERVICES RESPONDED IMMEDIATELY BY CONTACTING THE OPERATOR ON CALL. OPERATIONS RESPONDED TO LOCATION AND FOUND THAT THERE WERE HIGH O2 LEVELS COMING IN FROM THE FIELD. OPERATIONS IMMEDIATELY SHUT DOWN THE 101 INLET COMPRESSOR AND CONTACTED OXY FIELD PERSONNEL AND NOTIFIED THEM OF HIGH 02 LEVELS COMING IN FROM THE FIELD. FILED PERSONNEL CUT INLET INTO THE PLANT AND THE VRU WAS SHUT DOWN. DURING THIS TIME THE 202 REINJECTION COMPRESSOR SHUT DOWN ON HIGH LEVEL ON THE 4TH STAGE SCRUBBER. OPERATIONS DRAINED THE LIQUIDS AND PUT THE UNIT BACK ONLINE. THE 101 INLET COMPRESSOR REMAINS DOWN WHILE FIELD PERSONNEL LOCATE THE SOURCE OF THE O2 AND MAKE NECESSARY REPAIRS. ONCE THE O2 LEVEL IS DOWN TO AN ACCEPTABLE LEVEL THE 101 WILL BE PUT BACK ONLINE.
